Slammers Beach Volleyball

Goals

The Goal of the website is to grab the viewers attention in relation to colours, pictures and information. This will make them want to sign up and play volleyball.

Success Evaluation

The success of the site will be evaluated by the amount of viewers that have signed up to play volleyball meaning the site worked well in catching the viewers eye. With this the site gets hits and means that it has achieved what the client wanted.

Target Audience

The target audience for the site is teenagers because they are the ones more likely to get up and play volleyball as they find it fun. The site will be colourful and have pictures to show what it actually is to grab the teenager audience.
